About Me

I grew up in Charleston, South Carolina, and have always been passionate about technology and helping others use it effectively. As a designer and developer, I focus on creating engaging, user-friendly experiences. With a calm approach and commitment to continuous learning, I deliver quality work that meets both user needs and organizational goals. My research interests lie in human interaction with AI, such as Microsoft Copilot in Bing and how AI integration impacts diverse users in their everyday interactions with technology and services.
